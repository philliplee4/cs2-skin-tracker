How CS2 Skin Tracker Works

Stop manually refreshing skin marketplaces. Set your criteria once and get notified the moment a matching listing appears.

1

Create a free account

Sign up with a username, email and password. Your account stores your tracked items and notification settings so everything is saved between sessions.

2

Browse and find the skin you want

Use the navigation menu or search bar to find any CS2 skin — rifles, pistols, knives, gloves and more. Click a skin to open its detail page where you can see current market prices across Skinport and DMarket.

3

Set your tracking criteria

Click Track This Item on any skin page. You can set as many or as few filters as you want — or leave them blank to match any listing:

Price range — only notify within a min/max budget
Wear condition — Factory New, Field-Tested, etc.
Float value — precise float range for a specific look
StatTrak / Souvenir — require or exclude these variants
Pattern number — for specific seeds like Blue Gem #661
Doppler phase — Phase 2, Ruby, Sapphire, etc.

💡 The more specific your criteria, the fewer (but more relevant) notifications you'll get.
4

Connect your Discord for notifications

Go to your Profile page and scroll to Notification Settings. Paste a Discord webhook URL to receive match alerts directly in any Discord channel you choose.

💡 Don't have a webhook? In Discord: open a channel's settings → Integrations → Webhooks → New Webhook → Copy URL.
5

Get notified instantly when a match appears

We monitor Skinport's live listing feed in real time. The moment a new listing appears that matches your criteria, you'll get a Discord notification showing the price, float, pattern, and a direct link to the listing — before most people even know it's there.

When you first add a tracked item, we also scan all existing listings on Skinport and DMarket so you don't miss anything already available.

6

Manage your tracked items anytime

Visit your Profile page to see all your tracked items, current matches, and their status. You can edit criteria, cancel tracking, or filter your list by status, price, wear, and more at any time.

Common Questions

How fast are notifications sent?

For brand new listings on Skinport, notifications are sent within seconds of the listing appearing — we're connected to Skinport's live feed. For existing listings and DMarket, we scan every 20 minutes.

What markets do you track?

Currently Skinport (live feed + existing listings) and DMarket (existing listings). We're working on expanding to more markets.

Will I get spammed with notifications?

No. You only get notified when a genuinely new listing appears that matches your criteria. Already-seen listings don't trigger repeat notifications. If a match gets sold, you'll get one notification letting you know it's gone.

What if I don't set a price range?

We'll match any listing for that skin regardless of price. If you're tracking something common like an AK-47 Redline with no criteria, you'll get a lot of notifications. We recommend setting at least a max price.

How do I create a Discord webhook?

In Discord, right click the channel you want notifications in → Edit Channel → Integrations → Webhooks → New Webhook → Copy Webhook URL. Paste that URL in your Profile under Notification Settings.

Can I track the same skin with different criteria?

Yes — you can add the same skin multiple times with different settings. For example, track an AWP Dragon Lore as both Field-Tested under $5,000 and Factory New under $12,000 as separate entries.
